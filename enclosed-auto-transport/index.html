What is enclosed car transport?

With enclosed transport, your vehicle is shipped via a car carrier with a fully enclosed trailer. This shipping method offers higher security and protection. It's a great option for owners of luxury cars. Enclosed trailers can carry few vehicles at the same time, in most cases up to 7 vehicles.

What are enclosed car transport prices?

What's the price of an enclosed car shipping? Enclosed transport prices are around 30-40% higher than open transport, offering more protection per trip. For standard-sized vehicles: short routes (1-500 miles) average $709, medium routes (500-1500 miles) average $929 and longer routes (1500+ miles) average $1179.

Do I need enclosed car transport?

    Here are a few questions to ask yourself if you're deciding whether you need enclosed car transport, or the standard open auto transport:

  1. Do you consider your car an investment?
  2. Are you concerned about snow or cold weather?
  3. Are you concerned about dust, dirt, pollution, or other debris your car might be subject to over the course of transport?
  4. Is your car old and/or have a custom or unique paint job?
  5. Do you want extra protection against theft?

Peace-of-mind protection

We know that you want your car to arrive as clean as when you left it. Avoid exposure to the elements or extreme weather with enclosed auto shipping. Your enclosed car transport cost will just be a little bit extra.

Safe from rain, weather and road hazards

Enclosed car haulers keep your pride-and-joy protected from the elements. Most customers don't mind a little rain or snow but if you're dealing with an exotic or luxury car, covered vehicle shipping might be necessary for keeping your car dirt free.

Ship cars with low ground clearance

Hydraulic lift gates are necessary when loading and unloading a car with low ground clearance, and are only available with enclosed auto transport. When booking, make sure that you let our transport specialists know that it is a vehicle with low ground clearance so they will make sure that you receive an enclosed car shipper equipped with this feature.
